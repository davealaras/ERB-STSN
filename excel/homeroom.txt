For High School:
50% - Participation - with flexible number of measurable items depending on the adviser
30% - Written Output  -  with 3 of measurable items under it
20% - Attendance - auto-computed as Total # of Days Present / Total # of Class Days x 100 x 20%
          where the adviser encodes students' days absent on a monthly basis

For Grade School:
50% - Conduct - auto-computed based on the average of conduct grades given by each subject teacher including the homeroom adviser
          homeroom adviser also encodes conduct grade of students
40% - Involvement - - with 3 of measurable items under it
10% - Attendance - auto-computed as Total # of Days Present / Total # of Class Days x 100 x 20%
          where the adviser encodes students' days absent on a monthly basis
